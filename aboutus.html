About Us

Sometimes, you just need a little nudge, a little boost, a little concentration… that energy dose to keep you moving forward. You need Monster Energy.

Most companies spend their money on adds, radio spots, and billboards to try to tell you how good their products are. At Monster, we do things differently, because Monster Energy is not only a beverage. It's a lifestyle in a can.

We back up our scenes, groups, athletes and fans. We back up athletes so they can live from their passion. We promote concert tours so our favorite bands can play in your town. We celebrate with our fans and pilots by organizing the coolest parties and events we can think of. Monster Energy is the endless pursuit of victory, be at your best, be at the top of your art. It's the most badass energy drink on the planet. Monster Energy regroups the best skiers and skaters, riders and cyclists, rockers and pilots, players and girls in the world.
